sushi

  1. Cook rice in water
  2. chop filling into strips or sticks
  3. Mix sugar and vinegar
  4. spread out 1 sheet of nori
  5. Cover it as thinly as you can with the rice mixture
  6. lay out horizontal lines of your filling in nori
  7. roll up sushi as tight as you can
  8. get the rice-free strip of nori and glue it to the sushi roll
  9. Chop the roll into 1¼ slices youre done!
